# Airborne campaigns within AC3

The campaigns handled in the online description are:

- Arctic CLoud Observations Using airborne measurements during polar Day [ACLOUD](https://home.uni-leipzig.de/~ehrlich/ACLOUD_wiki_doku/doku.php)
- Airborne measurements of radiative and turbulent FLUXes of energy and momentum in the Arctic boundary layer [AFLUX](https://home.uni-leipzig.de/~ehrlich/AFLUX_wiki_doku/doku.php?id=start)
- MOSAiC Airborne observations in the Central Arctic [MOSAiC-ACA](https://home.uni-leipzig.de/~ehrlich/MOSAiC_ACA_wiki_doku/doku.php?id=start)
